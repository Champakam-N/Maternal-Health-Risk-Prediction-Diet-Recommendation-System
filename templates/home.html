Why Maternal Health Risk Assessment Matters

Maternal health risk assessment enables early identification of potential pregnancy-related complications. Monitoring vital parameters such as blood pressure, blood sugar, temperature, and heart rate supports timely clinical decisions and improved outcomes.

MamaCare leverages machine learning models to evaluate maternal health risks and provide personalized recommendations for preventive care.

  • Early detection of pregnancy complications
  • Personalized health recommendations
  • Regular monitoring of vital parameters
  • AI-powered risk assessment
  • Secure and private health tracking
